Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to build revenue by attracting new customers.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on). Target customers are anyone interested in white water rafting. They have the time and ability to travel, love the outdoors, and may or may not be part of an organized group. What do they want? They want to be able to find information to plan to go white water rafting. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will need to know dates trips are available, maximum group sizes, costs, and safety information as well as the ability to make a reservation.
